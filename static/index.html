FF2Disqus is a simple script for syncing comments between matching "threads" on Disqus and FriendFeed. Simply enter your credentials, select the appropriate Disqus site, match it to a FF service and click "Save Info". Once your information is saved any new comments that appear on your FF entries for the service you've chosen will be copied to Disqus. Currently this happens once an hour, but we provide a "ping" url if you'd like to sync more often. Most importantly, you can stop the syncing process at any time by selecting "Off" and saving.
